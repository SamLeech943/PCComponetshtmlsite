The Motherboard


The Motherboard is the largest PCB or Printed circuit board inside a compter system and acts as a link between all of the major components to bea able to send data to the CPU.
Motherboards are created with a few key components mainly, thhis consists of te circitory, the CPU socket, the RAM slots and the PCI-E slots for graphics cards.
Other Motherboards also come with additional features such as a heat sink which is a way of dispersing heat in the motherboard.
