The Computer Case


The case is a container to put all of the computer parts in to make sure they are not taking up too much space an to protect them from damage.
The case also has the job of supplying fans or other cooling solutions such as water cooling to stop the components from overheating.
Cases also come in various different sizes to accommodate different sizes of motherboard for smaller and larger builds.
